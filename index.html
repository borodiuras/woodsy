WoodsyBox

Convert your MPV into mini camper!

WoodsyBox lets you to convert your Multi Purpose Vehicle (MPV) into a mini camper. It has all you need for a comfy weekend escape – gas burner, fridge, running water, ample space for storage and fold-out bed for two! Designed to fit in most MPVs bootspace it still leaves rear seat for passenger use.
